# Welcome to SmartGrow

**Empowering the Next Generation of Precision Agriculture Innovators**

SmartGrow is a USDA-funded educational initiative that brings the power of precision agriculture into the homes and classrooms of middle school-aged youth. Through a hands-on **Smart Growing System (SGS)** and a structured **Smart Growing Curriculum (SGC)**, students explore the data-driven technologies reshaping U.S. farming.

---

## Why It Matters

U.S. agriculture faces mounting pressures — climate change, labor shortages, and rising production costs. Precision agriculture (also called digital or smart farming) offers proven solutions. But sustaining this innovation requires young people who understand and can apply smart technologies.

SmartGrow builds that foundation early, creating a pipeline of future talent equipped to lead the next wave of agricultural innovation.

---

## The Challenge We Address

> *"Precision agriculture plays a vital role in addressing key challenges in U.S. agriculture… it is essential to cultivate a next-generation workforce proficient in these smart technologies to ensure ongoing innovations and growth."*
> — Erickson et al., 2018; Kagan et al., 2022

Despite its importance, few K–12 programs introduce students to smart farming technologies in a meaningful, hands-on way. SmartGrow fills that gap.

---

## Our Approach

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Smart Growing System (SGS)** | A home-based growing system with real sensors that students set up, monitor, and analyze |
| **Smart Growing Curriculum (SGC)** | A structured learning program that builds foundational knowledge of smart technologies in an agricultural context |
| **STEM Identity Activities** | Outreach events and extension activities that connect students to real careers and innovators in the field |

---

## Project Goals

1. **Stimulate interest** in smart technologies among middle school youth
2. **Enhance Smart Technology Literacy (STL)** for agriculture and beyond
3. **Engage students** in design, execution, and evaluation using smart tools
4. **Foster STEM identity** through outreach and extension activities

---

## Funding & Alignment

SmartGrow is supported under the **USDA AFRI priority area: Agriculture Systems and Technology**, responding to documented national needs for a next-generation precision agriculture workforce.
