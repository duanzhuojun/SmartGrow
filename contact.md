## For Prospective Participants

If you are a student, parent, educator, or school administrator interested in joining the program, please include the following in your message:

- Your name and role (student, teacher, parent, administrator)
- Your location (state/region)
- Your affiliation (school name, 4-H club, community organization, etc.)
- A brief description of your interest in SmartGrow
