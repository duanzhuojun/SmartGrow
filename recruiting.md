# Get Involved

SmartGrow is actively recruiting participants, partner schools, and educators. Whether you are a parent, teacher, 4-H leader, or school administrator, there is a place for you in this project.

---

## Who We're Looking For

### Students (Middle School Youth, Ages 11–14)
We are recruiting enthusiastic middle schoolers who are curious about technology, plants, science, or farming. No prior experience is required — just a willingness to learn and explore!

**What students receive:**
- A complete home-based Smart Growing System (SGS) kit at no cost
- Access to the full Smart Growing Curriculum
- Mentorship from researchers and agricultural professionals
- Opportunities to present their findings at community events
- A head start on building their STEM identity

---

### Educators & 4-H Leaders
We are partnering with teachers, after-school program coordinators, and 4-H leaders to deliver the SGC in school and community settings.

**What partners receive:**
- Full curriculum materials and implementation support
- Professional development training
- Ongoing technical support from the SmartGrow team
- Opportunities to co-author publications and present at conferences

---

### Schools & Community Organizations
We welcome school districts, libraries, community centers, and youth organizations interested in bringing precision agriculture education to their communities.

---

## Eligibility

- Middle school-aged youth (grades 6–8, approximately ages 11–14)
- Located in the United States
- Access to a stable home environment for the growing system
- Parental/guardian consent for student participants
